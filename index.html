About Me

I'm a Principal Investigator and Researcher at the Ideker Lab, UCSD, where I work at the frontier of AI-driven biological research. My current focus is developing AI systems for autonomous research and co-scientist support in molecular biology and drug discovery.

I'm also pursuing a late-career doctorate in the Bioinformatics and Systems Biology program at UCSD, combining my extensive software development background with cutting-edge research.

As the creator and PI of the NDEx Project (ndexbio.org), I built "The Network Data Exchange" - a Network Commons that serves as the backbone for the Cytoscape ecosystem, enabling scientists worldwide to store, share, and publish biological networks.
